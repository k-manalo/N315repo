MISSION STATEMENT

This website is built for a client that wants to promote and sell space travel at a commercial level. Astrogate will allow users to browse a variety of voyages that travel through space similar to modern day cruise lines. Users will also be able to locate the nearest port to them using the map section. The site also sells a variety of space travel equipment that one might need when traversing the universe. This prototype demonstrates the potential for users to be able to create an account to manage their shopping experience much easier. The main color themes are blue/purple representing deep space, as well as orange in order to contrast the former colors. Because the main page is rather long I decided to keep the main navigation bar fixed the top of the screen. The navigation bar displays links for various other pages that could possibly be part of the site. There is also a FAQ section for usability purposes. The site theme could be considered niche, but I made it my goal to create something that stands out!

- Kyle Manalo
